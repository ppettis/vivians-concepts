Why Vivian's?

The name was on the corner long before it was on the sign.

The Vivian name is well known to many for the landmark Vivian's Plumbing business that for decades operated in Cottesloe, near the Boat Shed. Now the Vivian name re-emerges as a new, independent force in the local real estate sector.

A family-owned, boutique agency - founded by Gill Vivian, now led by her son Trent. Same family, same patch. A higher standard.
